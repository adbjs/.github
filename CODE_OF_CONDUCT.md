# Community Guidelines

This Code of Conduct provides community guidelines for a safe, respectful, productive, and collaborative place for any person who is willing to contribute to our community.

This Code of Conduct applies to all collaborative spaces, including communication channels (mailing lists, chat rooms, forums), code repositories, pull requests, commit comments, and community events.

## Guidelines

All participants in our community are expected to:

- **Be tolerant of opposing views**: Respect diverse perspectives and handle disagreements constructively.

- **Keep communication respectful**: Ensure that your language and actions are free of personal attacks and disparaging personal remarks.

- **Assume good intentions**: When interpreting the words and actions of others, always assume good intentions and seek clarification when needed.

- **No harassment**: Behavior which can be reasonably considered harassment will not be tolerated.

## Enforcement

Violations of this Code of Conduct may result in consequences including warnings, temporary bans, or permanent removal from the community. If you experience or witness violations, please report them to the project maintainers.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable
> behavior may be reported to the community leaders responsible for enforcement
> at [adbjs.contact@gmail.com](mailto:adbjs.contact@gmail.com). Appeals to enforcement
> decisions should also be directed to the email address listed. All complaints
> will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.
>
> All community leaders are obligated to respect the privacy and security of
> the reporter of any incident.
