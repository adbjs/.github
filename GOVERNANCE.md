# Governance

This project is **community-driven**, meaning the community is the driving force behind its development and maintenance. The project exists through the voluntary efforts of contributors who create, fix, and maintain various aspects of the organization.

Governed by teams of individuals with elevated permissions and responsibilities. These leadership teams are responsible for the overall direction of the organization, oversight across all repositories, and community moderation.

### Core

**Owners of the GitHub organisation.**

Responsible for organisation wide matters. Such as setting the direction of the organisation, approving new ports, adding new maintainers, and cultivating a healthy community.
